Spaghetti

Description

This recipe will guide you through making a classic spaghetti dish. It's simple, quick, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add spaghetti noodles and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Add tomato sauce to a pan and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and combine it with the sauce.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
