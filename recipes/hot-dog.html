Hot Dog

Description

This recipe will guide you through making a classic hot dog. It's quick, easy, and perfect for a summer barbecue or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog buns
  • Hot dogs
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Boil or grill the hot dogs until heated through.
  2. Toast the hot dog buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.
  3. Place the hot dog in the bun.
  4. Add your favorite condiments like ketchup, mustard, relish, and onions.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
